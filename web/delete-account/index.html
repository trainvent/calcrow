Delete Your Account

Last updated: March 12, 2026

If you created a Calcrow account, you can request permanent account deletion from inside the app. This page explains the expected deletion flow and what data is removed.

How to delete your Calcrow account

  1. Open the Calcrow app.
  2. Sign in to the account you want to delete.
  3. Open Settings.
  4. Choose the delete-account option and confirm the request.

What will be deleted

  • Your Calcrow account profile and authentication record.
  • Stored app settings associated with that account.
  • Linked cloud metadata saved specifically for your Calcrow account.

What is not automatically deleted

  • Spreadsheet files already stored on your device, in your browser downloads, or in your personal cloud storage.
  • Purchase, billing, or subscription records maintained by the app store, payment processor, or tax and accounting systems.
  • Backup copies retained temporarily for security, fraud prevention, or legal compliance where required.

Timing

Account deletion is intended to start immediately after confirmation. Some connected systems or retained records may take additional time to disappear from backups or provider dashboards.
